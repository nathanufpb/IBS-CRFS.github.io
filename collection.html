Collection Overview

The IBS-CRFS maintains one of the most comprehensive collections of soil fauna in the region. Our collection includes thousands of specimens representing diverse taxonomic groups found in soil ecosystems.

Preservation Methods

Our specimens are preserved using various methods appropriate to each taxonomic group:

  • Ethanol preservation (70-95%)
  • Microscope slide mounting
  • Dry preservation for selected groups
  • DNA-grade preservation for molecular studies

Access to the Collection

The collection is available for research purposes. Researchers interested in accessing our specimens should contact us through our contact page. We welcome collaborations and loan requests.

Collection Database

We maintain a detailed database of our collection, including:

  • Collection location and date
  • Habitat information
  • Collector information
  • Preservation methods
  • Associated data and images
