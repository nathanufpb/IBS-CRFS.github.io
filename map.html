About Our Sampling Sites

Our collection sites represent diverse ecosystems across different regions. Each location provides valuable insights into the distribution and diversity of soil fauna in various environmental conditions.

Site Information

For each sampling site, we record:

  • Geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude)
  • Elevation
  • Ecosystem type (forest, grassland, agricultural, etc.)
  • Soil characteristics
  • Sampling date and methodology
  • List of species collected
