Coleção de Referência da Fauna de Solos

Preserving and studying soil biodiversity for a sustainable future

Our Mission

The IBS-CRFS maintains a comprehensive reference collection of soil fauna, supporting research, education, and conservation efforts.

Research Excellence

Our collection serves as a vital resource for taxonomic studies, ecological research, and biodiversity assessments.

Collaboration

We work with researchers worldwide to advance the understanding of soil organisms and their ecological roles.

New Species Documented
