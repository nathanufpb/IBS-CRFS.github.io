Visit Us

Researchers are welcome to visit our facilities by appointment. Please contact us in advance to schedule your visit and discuss access to the collection.

Collaboration Opportunities

We are interested in developing collaborative research projects with other institutions. If you are interested in collaboration, please contact us with details about your proposed project or research interests.

Collection Loans

Specimen loans for research purposes can be arranged. Please submit your loan request by email, including:

  • List of requested specimens (with catalog numbers if available)
  • Purpose of the loan
  • Expected loan duration
  • Information about your institution and research project

Student Inquiries

Graduate students interested in developing research projects at IBS-CRFS should contact potential supervisors directly. Information about current research opportunities can be obtained by contacting our team members.
